# 📸 Quick Upload Guide - Unlimited File Size

## 🚀 How to Upload Images (No Size Limit!)

### Step 1: Access Upload Page
1. Login to millo as a **seller** or **admin**
2. Navigate to: **Upload Images** from dashboard
3. Or go directly to: `/upload-image.html`

### Step 2: Upload Your Images
**Method A: Drag & Drop**
- Drag images from your computer
- Drop them on the upload zone
- Done!

**Method B: Browse Files**
- Click "Browse Files" button
- Select one or multiple images
- Click "Open"
- Done!

### Step 3: Use Your Images
1. Click on any uploaded image to view it
2. Click "Copy URL" button
3. Paste URL in product listing "Image URL" field
4. Your product now has a high-quality image!

## 🎯 Quick Tips

💡 **Best Practices:**
- Use descriptive filenames (e.g., "red-tshirt-front.jpg")
- Upload high-resolution images for best quality
- No need to compress or resize before upload
- Upload all product photos at once to save time

⚠️ **What to Avoid:**
- Don't upload non-image files (PDFs, videos, etc.)
- Don't use special characters in filenames
- Don't upload copyrighted images without permission

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

**Problem:** Upload button not working  
**Solution:** Make sure you're logged in as seller or admin

**Problem:** Large file takes long time  
**Solution:** This is normal! Large files need more time. Wait for completion.

**Problem:** Can't see uploaded images  
**Solution:** Refresh the page or check if you're viewing correct account

**Problem:** Upload fails  
**Solution:** Check internet connection, try again, or contact support
